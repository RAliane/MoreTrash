# Cookie Policy for Matchgorithm

**Effective Date**: 2026-01-14

## 1. What Are Cookies
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website.

## 2. How We Use Cookies
We use essential cookies for:
- **Authentication**: Keep you logged in securely
- **Security**: Protect against fraud and abuse
- **Preferences**: Remember your settings
- **Analytics**: Improve our service (with consent)

## 3. Cookie Categories

### Essential Cookies (Always Active)
| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| session_id | Maintain secure login | Session |
| csrf_token | Prevent cross-site attacks | Session |
| consent_status | Remember cookie preferences | 1 year |

### Functional Cookies (With Consent)
| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| theme_preference | Remember dark/light mode | 1 year |
| language | Remember language choice | 1 year |

### Analytics Cookies (With Consent)
| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| _ga | Google Analytics | 2 years |
| _gid | Google Analytics | 24 hours |

## 4. Cookie Consent
- **Banner**: Appears on first visit
- **Granular Control**: Accept/reject by category
- **Easy Withdrawal**: Change preferences anytime
- **No Tracking Without Consent**: Analytics disabled by default

## 5. Third-Party Cookies
We may use:
- **Google Analytics**: Website analytics (with consent)
- **Stripe**: Payment processing (checkout only)
- **Intercom**: Customer support (with consent)

## 6. UK Cookie Law Compliance
- **Cookie Audit**: Regular compliance reviews
- **User Control**: Easy opt-out mechanisms
- **Data Minimization**: Only necessary cookies
- **Transparency**: Full cookie disclosure

## 7. Managing Cookies
- **Browser Settings**: Block cookies in browser
- **Cookie Banner**: Use our consent management
- **Account Settings**: Manage preferences
- **Contact Us**: Request cookie deletion

## 8. Updates to This Policy
We will notify you of significant changes via email.
